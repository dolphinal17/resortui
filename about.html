About us 10 Waves Water Park and Hotel has been a beloved destination for water park enthusiasts for 23 years. Since our opening in 2000, we have been committed to providing a thrilling, safe, and family-friendly environment for our guests to enjoy. Over the years, we have continued to expand and innovate, adding new attractions and amenities to keep up with the latest trends in water park entertainment.

We are proud to have become a staple of the local community and a must-visit destination for tourists from around the world. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to providing the best possible experience for our guests, with a focus on safety, fun, and unforgettable memories. Thank you for choosing 10 Waves Water Park and Hotel as your destination for aquatic adventure!
