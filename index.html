Welcome to

Situated on the pristine shores of a tranquil ocean, our resort offers breathtaking views of the horizon and the rhythmic sound of waves that will lull you into a state of complete relaxation. At 10Waves, we offer an array of activities, from surfing and water sports to yoga and spa treatments, ensuring there's something for everyone. Whether you're seeking adventure or simply looking to unwind, we invite you to experience the magic of 10Waves and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

10Waves resort was founded in 2000 by surf enthusiasts on a pristine coastline with a handful of bungalows and a small surf shop, and over the years, it has grown to include additional villas, a restaurant, and a variety of exciting activities such as yoga, water sports, and spa treatments. Til this day, it continues to inspire and captivate visitors from around the world as one of the top destinations for surfers and adventure seekers.
